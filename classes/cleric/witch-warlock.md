<span style="color: yellow">**Alignment**:</span> <span style="color: white">Malice</span>
{: .lh-tight}

These dark shamans practice an ancient form of magic considered evil and horrible by most. They use the skulls of their dead victims and link the dark power of their gods to them, evoking horrid affects on the victim.

<span style="color: yellow">**Fighting style**:</span> <span style="color: white">One-handed; usually a spear or dagger.</span>
{: .lh-tight}

<span style="color: yellow">**Skills**:</span> <span style="color: white">skull, spear</span>
{: .lh-tight}

<span style="color: yellow">**Spells**:</span> <span style="color: white">blindness, counterspell, curse, demonfire, dispel good, fly, mass healing, plague, poison, third eye</span>
{: .lh-tight}
